.012 NITI and Bonded Eyelets
I’ve discussed before how much I LOVE .012 NITI! I use it 99.9% of the time as my initial arch wire. It aligns teeth super-fast and I feel like it is less painful for the patient as well.
Combined with bondable eyelets, I’ve seen some amazing results soon after the initial bonding!
Case AM Pre Treatment
Case AM After 16 weeks (2 visits)
Note on the above case that Large Opening Eyelets were used on Lower 4’s, Lower Left 2, and Small Opening Eyelet was used on Lower Right 1. Also, the 2nd Photo shows a .016 NITI that was just placed prior to taking photo.
Things to be aware of with .012 as your initial arch wire:
  • I rarely engage the 6’s as the wire is so flexible it almost guarantees an emergency wire out.
  • If patients chew ice the wire can easily freeze and fracture.
